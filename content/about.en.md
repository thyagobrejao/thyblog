## Hi, I'm Thyago! 👋

I'm a technology enthusiast who learned to program **self-taught** in **2000**, when I was just **16 years old**. That teenage curiosity about understanding how things work led me to dive into the world of programming, and I've never stopped since.

## Education and Career

I graduated in **Systems Analysis in 2006**, building on the knowledge I had been developing on my own. Today I work as a **Software Engineer**, but my main profession is **Federal Highway Police Officer** — an unusual combination that gives me unique perspectives in both the technology world and public service.

- 🎯 **Airsoft** — Strategy, tactics, and plenty of adrenaline

## Home Lab & Automation

At home, I maintain a **home lab server** running several **open source** systems. The centerpiece is **[Home Assistant](https://www.home-assistant.io/)**, which serves as the brain for all my home automations. I integrated security cameras using **[Frigate](https://frigate.video/)** for intelligent object and event detection, all working seamlessly and automated.
